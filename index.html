All-Inclusive Resort with Lake Front Luxury Cabins on Blackduck Lake

This BRAND NEW ALL-INCLUSIVE family resort now offers vacation homes and cabin rentals on the North shore of Blackduck Lake. The resort offers some of the most amazing sunsets on the lake year round. There are small 1 bedroom cabins all the way to a 6 bedroom cabin that sleeps up to 22 people. Going up north to spend a week at the lake is a big part of Minnesota tradition, and a stay at White Birch Resort is the perfect way to experience that.

The White Birch Resort experience is for those individuals who seek and appreciate a well-deserved taste of luxury resorts while on vacation. With a relaxed approach to luxury for groups of all ages and sizes, guests are excited and guaranteed to enjoy 2600 acres of four season's recreational adventure to explore, accompanied with endless and unmatched resort amenities minus the crowds. As many guests comment, "we feel as though we are in our own private custom lake home with beautiful refined style and decor."

Our Cabins

White Birch Resort has 10 BRAND NEW high end custom built vacation home and cabins, ranging from 640 sq. ft. one room studio cabins to deluxe 3600 sq. ft. six bedroom entertainment reunion homes for up to 22 guests. All 10 deluxe size accommodations can host groups of up to 128 guests on the resort year round. Guests can choose from any of the beautifully designed rental home and lake cabin rental MN ranging from a “budget friendly” one room studio cabin to multiple expansive six bedroom private family entertainment reunion homes.

Cabin #1

This Minnesota family Reunion Home with 6 Bedrooms, 3 Bathrooms and FREE Pontoon Boat(most dates) is built for large groups. Designed to hold family reunions or large groups looking for a private vacation home, the cabin can sleep up to 22 people.

There is over 3600 sq ft of living space spread throughout 3 floors, covered decks and screened in porch. The main floor has the chefs kitchen, living room, dining room, master bath, master bedroom, pay laundry room, screened in porch and more.

The cabin features all the modern amenities of a private home. There is a full kitchen with 2 side by side refrigerators, double oven, all utensils and kitchenware needed for 22 guests. It has central air, picnic table, log swing and a fishing boat included.

Outside there is a private fire pit with free firewood, gas grill, Adirondack Chairs, picnic bench, wood swing, front yard for playing and more.
